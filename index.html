How to use Memrise uploade

To use this add-on you just need to go to one of your courses database page and scroll till the bottom until yuo find a multiple input form with a button. Choose your file and click upload, Firefox could stops for some seconds but there's nothing wrong with that.

F.A.Q

  • Does this add-on upload all the files I'm throwing at it?

    No, it just search for those word without audio, the next version will let you choose to upload everything in addition.
